Sampung Salita na Hindi Gaanong Ginagamit ng mga Filipino

Muni-Muni (To think Deeply)
The act of deep contemplation or reflection
Pantablay (Charger)
A device used for charging storage bateries
Hambog (Arrogant)
An insulting attitude of people who belive they are better than others
Sakinsukob (Universe)
Entirety of space and time including planets, stars and matter
Batlag (Car)
Self powered motor vehicle used for transportation
Alimusom (Fragrant)
A sweet or delicate odor
Dumatal (Arrived)
Reached a place or at the end of a journey
Panginain (Browser)
A program that is used to find and look at information in the internet
Payneta (Comb)
A stip of platic with a row narrow teeth, used for untangling or arranging the hair
