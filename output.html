Sampung Salita na Hindi Gaanong Ginagamit ng mga Taong Filipino

Marahuyo

Definition: The feeling of being completely absorbed by someone's beauty, a stunning view, or an exceptional performance.

Example: Namarahuyo ako sa ganda ng tanawin sa taas ng bundok.

Muni-muni

Definition: The act of deep contemplation or reflection.

Example: Madalas siyang mag-muni-muni sa tabi ng dagat para makapag-isip nang malinaw.

Pantablay

Definition: A device used for charging storage batteries.

Example: Bumili ng bagong pantablay si Francis para sa kanyang cellphone.

Hambog

Definition: An insulting attitude of people who believe they are better than others.

Example: Napakahambog ni Eric kapag kausap ang kanyang mga kaklase.

Sansinukob

Definition: It refers to the whole entirety of space and time including planets, stars, galaxies, matter, and energy.

Example: Ang sansinukob na kinalalagyan natin ay punong-puno ng mga bituin.

Batlag

Definition: A wheeled, self-powered motor vehicle used for transportation and a product of the automotive industry.

Example: Naku, nakabangga daw yung batlag na bago mong bili dun sa kanto!

Alimusom

Definition: A sweet or delicate odor.

Example: Nakagagaan ng loob ang alimusom ng mga bulaklak.

Dumatal

Definition: Reach a place at the end of a journey or a stage in a journey.

Example: Dumatal na ang in-order mong pagkain sa McDonalds.

Panginain

Definition: A computer program that is used to find and look at information on the Internet.

Example: Ang gamit niya na panginain ay Google Chrome.

Payneta

Definition: A strip of plastic, metal, or wood with a row of narrow teeth, used for untangling or arranging the hair.

Example: Ang payneta ni Anna ay marumi.
